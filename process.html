No Mysteries, Just a Clear Path Forward.

This is my entire process from start to finish. I believe in total transparency, so you know exactly what to expect at every stage of your automation project.

Step 1

The Discovery Call

This is our initial, no-pressure chat. The entire goal is to find your single biggest operational headache. We'll talk about the repetitive tasks you hate doing, where you feel like you're losing time, and what an "ideal" day would look like for you. We map out the problem together.

Step 2

The Strategy & Proposal

After our call, I'll send you a straightforward proposal. It will detail the exact workflow I'll build, a visual map of how it works, the specific apps we'll connect, and a fixed price. It will also outline the 50/50 payment structure. For maximum security and convenience, all payments and invoicing are processed through our trusted partner, Stripe. No hidden fees, no surprises.

Step 3

The Build & Integration

Once you approve the proposal and submit the 50% deposit, I get to work. I'll start building the custom workflow that connects your apps (like your calendar, email, and CRM) so they can work for you automatically. You can just focus on your customers while I handle all the technical setup.

Step 4

Testing & Handoff

Before I hand anything over, I rigorously test the system to make sure it's running perfectly. Once it's ready, I'll record a personalized video walkthrough just for you. For clients on Oahu, I'll personally deliver the system and walk you through it on-site. For larger projects, I'm also available to travel to the outer islands. For everyone else, you'll get a detailed video explaining exactly what I built and how it works.

Step 5

Go-Live & Support

After you've reviewed the video and given the green light, we'll turn the system on. The final 50% invoice is sent at this time. For the next 30 days, I'm on standby to fix any unexpected bugs or make minor tweaks to ensure everything runs smoothly. Your success is my success.

My Tool of Choice

I specialize in building powerful, self-hosted automation systems using n8n. This approach gives you maximum control and privacy over your data, without being locked into expensive monthly subscriptions.
