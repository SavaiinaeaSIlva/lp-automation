Refund & Cancellation Policy

Effective Date: August 27, 2025

1. Deposit Policy

All project deposits (50% of total project cost) are non-refundable. This covers initial planning, resource allocation, and opportunity costs.

2. Cancellation Terms

  • Before Work Begins: If Client cancels after Proposal acceptance but before work starts, only the deposit is retained.
  • After Work Begins: If Client cancels after work has commenced, no refunds are provided for completed portions of work.

3. Refund Limitations

Refunds are not issued once project deliverables have been completed and provided to the Client.

4. Project Rescheduling

Clients may reschedule project timelines once without penalty if written notice is provided at least 7 business days in advance.

5. Dispute Resolution

Refund disputes are handled under the Dispute Resolution process outlined in our Terms & Conditions.

6. Emergency Circumstances

In extraordinary circumstances (such as Consultant inability to perform due to force majeure), refunds will be considered on a case-by-case basis for work not yet completed.
