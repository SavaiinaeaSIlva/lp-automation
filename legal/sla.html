Service Level Agreement

Effective Date: September 3, 2025

This SLA is an addendum to our main Service Agreement and Terms & Conditions.

This Service Level Agreement ("SLA") is entered into between Silva Automation ("Provider") and the client ("Client") as an addendum to the primary Service Agreement. This document outlines our commitments to service quality, response times, and performance standards.

1. Service Commitments

Response Times

  • Initial Response: Within 24 hours during business days (Monday-Friday, 8:00 AM - 5:00 PM HST)
  • Emergency Issues: Within 4 hours during business days
  • Project Updates: Weekly status reports or as agreed in Project Proposal

Availability Standards

  • Website/System Uptime: 99.5% monthly uptime for delivered systems
  • Support Availability: Monday-Friday, 8:00 AM - 5:00 PM HST, excluding Hawaii state holidays
  • Planned Maintenance: Scheduled during off-peak hours with 48-hour notice

Performance Standards

  • Project Delivery: As specified in Project Proposal timeline
  • Quality Assurance: All deliverables tested before client handover
  • Documentation: Complete documentation provided with all deliverables

2. Service Credits

If we fail to meet the specified service levels, Client may be eligible for service credits:

  • Uptime below 99.5%: 5% credit of monthly service fee
  • Response time violations: 2% credit per incident
  • Missed delivery deadlines: As negotiated per project

3. Exclusions

This SLA does not apply to:

  • Issues caused by client actions or third-party services
  • Force majeure events
  • Scheduled maintenance windows
  • Internet or telecommunications provider failures

4. Escalation Procedures

Issue Escalation Path:

  1. Level 1: Initial support contact via email or phone
  2. Level 2: Escalation to senior technical staff within 4 hours
  3. Level 3: Management involvement for critical issues
  4. Level 4: Executive escalation for business-critical problems

5. Performance Monitoring

We continuously monitor our service performance and will provide monthly reports on SLA compliance. Performance metrics include response times, resolution times, system uptime, and customer satisfaction scores.

6. Service Improvement

We are committed to continuous improvement of our services. Regular reviews of SLA performance help us identify areas for enhancement and ensure we meet or exceed our commitments to clients.
