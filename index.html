Frequently Asked Questions

The Value & The Results

What kind of businesses do you work with?

We specialize in helping Hawaii-based service businesses (e.g., junk removal, landscaping, cleaning services, contractors). If you're feeling buried in administrative work, we can build a system for you.

What problems do you solve?

We solve the feeling of being overwhelmed and trapped by your own business. If you're spending nights and weekends on admin tasks, losing track of new leads, or feel like you can't step away without things falling apart, we build the systems that run those tasks for you, automatically.

What results can I realistically expect?

Our clients experience four key results: Freedom (more time), Scalability (growth without more hiring), Professionalism (five-star client experiences), and Peace of Mind (your business runs 24/7).

The Process & The Partnership

What's the process from start to finish?

Most projects are designed, built, and delivered within 7-14 days. After our initial call, we map out your exact needs, build the custom system, then deliver the finished product and train you and your team.

What does the training and handover look like?

This is our local advantage. If your business is on Oahu or any of the neighbor islands, we come to you. We provide in-person, hands-on training for you and your key staff to ensure everyone is confident using the new system.

Is this a monthly subscription?

No. Our automation packages are a one-time investment. Once we build your system, you own it outright.

What isn't included in my project?

Our service is focused on building your automation system. We are not Virtual Assistants (VAs)—the systems run themselves, but we don't perform the daily tasks for you. The project fee also does not include the separate subscription costs for any software you use (like your CRM, etc.).

Support & Technical Questions

What tools do you use?

We primarily use powerful, flexible platforms like n8n. This allows us to create robust automations that can be self-hosted, which keeps your long-term costs low and gives you full ownership of your data.

What support is included with a standard project?

Every project includes a 90-day support window to fix any bugs or issues that arise with the original system we built for you.

What does "1 Year of Premium Support" include?

Our Founding Clients receive an all-inclusive support package for a full year: bug fixes, troubleshooting, up to one hour of tweaks per month, and on-call access for when you need help.
