Privacy Policy

Effective Date: August 29, 2025

This Privacy Policy describes how Silva Automation ("we," "us," or "our") collects, uses, and discloses information from users of our website and services, located at https://ssilva.space/ . We are committed to protecting your privacy and complying with all applicable laws, including the data protection principles of the State of Hawaii.

1. Information We Collect

We collect information to provide and improve our services and to communicate with you. The types of information we may collect include:

  • Personal Information: This includes data you voluntarily provide to us via contact forms, project inquiries, or scheduling software. This may include your name, company name, email address, phone number, and any other information you provide in your communications.
  • Payment Information: We do not directly collect or store your payment card details. All payment transactions are processed securely through a third-party payment processor, such as PayPal or Stripe. We only receive a notification of a successful payment and information necessary to verify the transaction.
  • Project and Service Data: We collect and process data required to perform our automation services, which may include customer relationship management (CRM) data, marketing campaign data, and other business information necessary to complete a project. This data is handled with strict confidentiality.
  • Usage and Technical Data: When you visit our website, we automatically collect certain information about your device and your activity. This includes your IP address, browser type, device type, operating system, and data about your interaction with our site, such as pages viewed and time spent.
  • Cookies and Tracking Technologies: We use cookies and similar technologies to enhance your user experience, analyze site usage, and improve our services. Our Cookie Policy provides more detail on how we use these technologies and how you can manage them.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect for the following purposes:

  • To provide, operate, and maintain our services.
  • To communicate with you, including responding to your inquiries and sending you service-related and promotional information.
  • To process payments and complete transactions.
  • To analyze and understand how our website is used, which helps us improve our services and marketing efforts.
  • To maintain the security of our website and services and to detect and prevent fraud.
  • To comply with our legal obligations, including fulfilling requests from government authorities or court orders.

3. Sharing and Disclosure of Information

We do not sell your personal information. We may share or disclose your information in the following limited circumstances:

  • Service Providers: We may share information with third-party vendors and service providers who perform services on our behalf, such as payment processors, website analytics providers (e.g., Google Analytics), and email marketing services. These providers are only given access to information necessary to perform their functions and are contractually obligated to protect your information.
  • Business Transfers: In the event of a merger, acquisition, or sale of assets, your information may be transferred as part of that transaction.
  • Legal Compliance and Protection: We may disclose your information if required to do so by law or in the good faith belief that such action is necessary to comply with a legal obligation, protect our rights or property, or ensure the safety of our users or the public.

4. Data Security and Breach Notification

We implement reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect your information from unauthorized access, loss, or alteration. We also comply with Hawaii Revised Statutes ยง 487N, which requires us to notify you in the event of a security breach involving your personal information.

5. Your Rights and Choices

Subject to applicable law, you have the following rights regarding your information:

  • Access: You can request access to the personal information we hold about you.
  • Correction: You can request that we correct any inaccurate or incomplete information.
  • Deletion: You can request the deletion of your personal information, subject to certain exceptions (e.g., for legal retention purposes).
  • Opt-Out: You can opt out of receiving marketing communications from us by following the unsubscribe instructions in the emails you receive.

To exercise any of these rights, please contact us at privacy@ssilva.space.

6. Data Retention

We retain your personal information for as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes for which it was collected, including for the purposes of satisfying any legal, accounting, or reporting requirements. Our general retention periods are:

  • Project-related data: Retained for the duration of the service agreement plus three (3) years.
  • Website analytics data: Retained for 26 months.
  • Financial records: Retained for seven (7) years to comply with tax and accounting requirements.
  • Marketing data: Retained until you opt out.

7. Cookies and Third-Party Links

Our website uses cookies. You can manage your cookie preferences through your browser settings. Our website may also contain links to third-party websites or services. We are not responsible for the privacy practices of these third parties.

8. Children's Privacy

Our website and services are not intended for children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

9. International Users

Our business is located in Oahu, Hawaii, USA. If you are accessing our services from outside the United States, your information will be processed and stored in the U.S., where our servers and databases are located. By using our services, you consent to the transfer of your information to the U.S.

10. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will post any changes on this page with a new effective date. Your continued use of our services after the changes are made will be considered acceptance of those changes.
